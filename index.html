Bean Points

Your loyalty means a lot to us and we'd like to thank you through our Bean Points system.

For every coffee you buy, we'll give you back 2 Bean Points. Once you've collected 12 Bean Points (or 6 coffees), we'll reward with a free coffee of your choosing. It's easy to get started:

  • Collect a Bean Points loyalty card on your next visit
  • Get your loyalty card stamped after each coffee you purchase
  • Watch your Bean Points add up
  • Collect your free coffee once you've reached 12 points

About
