Coffee to Go

We're delighted to launch our new 'Coffee to Go' service in Blackrock. We have our full range of coffees available for takeaway, including our most popular Leafy Bean Signature Caramel Macchiato!

Our takeaway coffees are still being prepared by our expert baristas, using only the highest quality coffee beans. So your takeaway coffee will taste just as delicious as those in-store.

Bean Points

Your loyalty means a lot to us and we'd like to thank you through our Bean Points system.

For every coffee you buy, we'll give you back 2 Bean Points. Once you've collected 12 Bean Points (or 6 coffees), we'll reward with a free coffee of your choosing. It's easy to get started:

  • Collect a Bean Points loyalty card on your next visit
  • Get your loyalty card stamped after each coffee you purchase
  • Watch your Bean Points add up
  • Collect your free coffee once you've reached 12 points

About

The Leafy Bean is a lively coffee shop located in the heart of Blackrock village. We're very passsionate coffee which you'll see from our expert baristas to our high quality coffee beans.
